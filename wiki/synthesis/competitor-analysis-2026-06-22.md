# Competitor Analysis: Adar Realty Studios

## Question
What does the competitive landscape look like for Adar Realty Studios in the AI automation for real estate agents market?

## Key Findings
- Biggest threat: Lofty launched Agentic AI OS in Feb 2026 — full-stack autonomous AI for teams/brokers
- Biggest opportunity: Every major competitor targets teams — solo agents are almost completely ignored
- AI voice and text automation is table stakes; agents without it lose leads to instant-response competitors
- Proptech AI funding up 42% annually — competition will intensify in the next 12–18 months
- The done-for-you agency model is Adar's clearest differentiation — no competitor does this

## Competitors Analyzed
- **Lofty** — Full agentic AI OS, teams/brokers, $500–$1,500+/mo est.
- **Ylopo** — AI lead gen + voice/text nurturing, 75k users, ~$500–$600/mo
- **Structurely** — AI voice + SMS qualification, ~$149–$500/mo, multi-industry
- **Follow Up Boss** — CRM with AI routing, top 41/50 US teams, $69–$499/mo
- **Luxury Presence** — AI marketing platform, luxury agents, premium pricing
- **SmartZip** — Predictive seller lead analytics, $300–$600/mo est.

## Top Opportunities
- Solo agent package at $299–399/mo — undercuts all competitors, targets biggest underserved segment
- AI Voice Receptionist as standalone add-on — no competitor sells this as a buy-it-now product
- Instagram DM automation — completely unaddressed by major players
- Done-for-you model vs. every competitor's DIY SaaS

## Recommended Actions
1. Launch $299–399/mo Solo Agent Package
2. Productize AI Voice Receptionist at $199/mo as standalone add-on
3. Build one case study with hard numbers (leads contacted, appointments booked, hours saved)
4. SEO comparison pages: "Ylopo alternative for solo agents", "Follow Up Boss without the setup"
5. Instagram content series: before/after of leads with vs. without automation
