**Timeline:** June 2026 - August 2026 (Proof of Concept Phase)

## Executive Summary

Build AI automation agency focused on real estate agents. Leverage existing relationship with Sohail Salhadin (eXp Realty group leader, family connection) to gain market entry, validate product-market fit, and close 1-3 paying customers before relocating to Israel end of August.

## Market Opportunity

### Chicago Real Estate Market
- ~15,000 active agents in Chicago metro
- 5-person teams are common unit size
- Typical pain: Lead follow-up (10+ hrs/week), scheduling chaos, CRM data entry

### Target Customer Profile
- **Primary:** Independent agents or small teams (3-10 agents)
- **Secondary:** Group leaders (like Sohail) managing multiple agents
- **Budget:** $5-15K/month automation retainer
- **Pain:** Follow-up, scheduling, CRM, communication coordination

### Market Entry Strategy
1. **Warm intro:** Sohail Salhadin (knows family, just reconnected)
2. **Referrals:** Sohail's network (5-10+ agents/brokers)
3. **Direct:** Cold outreach to Lakefront/Wicker Park (backup)

---

## Product/Service

### What We Sell
Automation suite for RE agents:
- Lead follow-up sequences (automated, personalized)
- Showing scheduling (calendar sync, no conflicts)
- CRM auto-population (email→CRM, task tracking)
- Commission tracking (by agent, real-time)
- Client communication (consistent touchpoints)

### Pricing Model
- **Retainer:** $5-15K/month (based on team size, features)
- **Typical:** 5-person team = $8-10K/month
- **Larger:** 10+ person group = $12-15K/month
- **Scaling:** Add agents = +$1-2K/month per agent

### Value Prop
**For agents:** Save 10+ hours/week on admin, close more deals through consistent follow-up
**For brokers:** Agent retention, scalable team management, performance visibility
