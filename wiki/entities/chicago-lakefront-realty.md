## Their Pain Points

- **Lead follow-up:** Agents manually texting/emailing leads. No consistent follow-up system. Leads fall through cracks.
- **Calendar chaos:** Scheduling showings via email threads. Double-bookings happen. No central calendar.
- **CRM data entry:** Agent notes scattered across email, phone notes, MLS. No single source of truth.
- **Document prep:** Agents spend 2-3 hours on contract paperwork for each deal. Could be templated.
- **Time spent on admin:** Agents spend 15+ hours/week on follow-up + admin instead of selling.

## Our Fit

**Problem:** 5 agents × 15 hours/week on admin = 75 hours/week wasted. At $500/hour value = $37,500/week opportunity cost.

**Solution:** Automation that handles:
1. Lead capture → automated follow-up sequences
2. CRM auto-population from emails
3. Calendar sync for all showings
4. Document templating for contracts

**Outcome:** Free up 10+ hours/week per agent. More time closing deals. Better client experience.

**Deal size:** $5,000-$8,000/month retainer (8-12% of lead follow-up time saved)
