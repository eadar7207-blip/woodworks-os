## Their Pain Points

- **Lead follow-up:** Buyers interested but no consistent contact. First-time buyers need hand-holding.
- **Showing scheduling:** Back-and-forth texts with clients for appointment times. Manual calendar updates.
- **Client communication:** Inconsistent touchpoints. Some buyers go silent mid-transaction.
- **Data organization:** Buyer info scattered across emails and notes. Hard to reference buyer preferences.
- **Follow-up on lost leads:** Buyers who went cold could be re-engaged with nurture sequence.

## Our Fit

**Problem:** With first-time buyer focus, client handholding is critical. Manual follow-up = leads go cold. With 5 agents, that's ~8 lost leads/month × $3,500 avg commission = $140K/year lost.

**Solution:**
1. Automated buyer nurture sequences (mortgage pre-approval → property matching → closing)
2. Showing scheduling automation (instant calendar sync)
3. Buyer portal for document uploads + status updates
4. Lead re-engagement campaigns

**Outcome:** Close 15-20% more leads through consistent follow-up. Better buyer experience = referrals.

**Deal size:** $6,000-$10,000/month (highly scalable with their growth focus)
