# 15 Powerful Prompts Realtors Should Use
## Instagram Carousel Series for @eitanadar.ai

---

## Carousel 1: Lead Qualification
**Title:** "The 3-Question Prompt Every Agent Should Use to Qualify Leads"

**Prompts:**
1. "Analyze this lead's profile and tell me: (1) Are they a serious buyer or browser? (2) What's their likely budget range? (3) What's the best next step to move them forward? Lead info: [paste lead details]"

2. "I just got 50 new leads. Give me a ranking of the top 10 based on likelihood to close. Here's the data: [paste]"

3. "Write a personalized follow-up email to this lead based on their behavior. They visited 5 properties but haven't responded to my calls. Lead: [paste]"

---

## Carousel 2: Proposal Generation
**Title:** "Generate Custom Proposals in 2 Minutes (Not 2 Hours)"

**Prompts:**
1. "Create a professional real estate proposal for a [property type] in [area]. Client: [name]. Price: [price]. Key selling points: [list]. Make it impressive but concise."

2. "Write a compelling executive summary for this listing that emphasizes ROI for investors. Property: [details]. Target investor type: [type]"

3. "Generate 3 different proposal angles for the same property targeting different buyer personas: investor, family homebuyer, and first-time buyer."

---

## Carousel 3: Email Sequences
**Title:** "5 Email Templates That Actually Get Responses"

**Prompts:**
1. "Write a follow-up email sequence for prospects who viewed properties but haven't made offers. [Prospect context]. Make it feel personal, not salesy."

2. "Create an educational email that positions me as a market expert and builds trust. Topic: [your local market]. Include stats and insights."

3. "Write a win-back email for clients who went cold 6 months ago. Make them feel valued, not pressured."

---

## Carousel 4: CRM Organization
**Title:** "Keep Your CRM Clean With These Prompts"

**Prompts:**
1. "Analyze my CRM data and tell me: How many leads are stale? Which should get follow-up calls today? What deals are close to closing? [Paste summary]"

2. "Create a follow-up schedule for my 30 active prospects. Categorize by urgency and suggest contact frequency for each."

3. "Write clear notes templates for my CRM so I don't forget critical details. Include: initial contact, property interest, objections, next steps."

---

## Carousel 5: Listing Descriptions
**Title:** "AI-Written Listing Descriptions That Sell Homes"

**Prompts:**
1. "Write a compelling listing description for this property. Make it shine but honest. Details: [address, features, recent upgrades]. Target buyer: [type]"

2. "Create 3 variations of a listing headline. Property: [type] in [neighborhood]. Price: [price]. Make each appeal to different buyer personas."

3. "Write a story about this house — what lifestyle does it offer? Property: [details]. Avoid clichés."

---

## Carousel 6: Negotiation Scripts
**Title:** "Scripts for Common Negotiation Scenarios"

**Prompts:**
1. "Help me craft a response to this low-ball offer. Property value: [price]. Offer: [price]. Market conditions: [context]. What's my counter-offer strategy?"

2. "Write a script for handling a seller who's emotional about their home. I need to be empathetic but professional."

3. "Create talking points for convincing a seller to reduce price and get the home sold faster vs. holding out for higher price."

---

## Carousel 7: Market Analysis
**Title:** "Instant Market Reports (Without Hours of Research)"

**Prompts:**
1. "Analyze the [neighborhood] real estate market. Give me: avg price trends last 12 months, days on market, buyer profiles, emerging trends. Support with realistic data."

2. "Compare 3 neighborhoods for my clients. They want [type of property]. Give me analysis on: price per sq ft, appreciation rates, schools, commute times."

3. "Create a weekly market update I can send to my sphere of influence. Focus on: what's happening locally, what it means for buyers/sellers, action items."

## How to Use These Prompts

**For Quick Wins:**
- Copy a prompt directly into ChatGPT, Claude, or your AI tool
- Fill in [bracketed information]
- Paste the output into your CRM/email/documents
- Adjust tone/length as needed

**For Consistency:**
- Save your favorite prompts
- Create templates with your common info pre-filled
- Build a library organized by use case

**For Leverage:**
- Use these with your CRM to automate follow-ups
- Combine with email sequences for consistent outreach
- Generate bulk content for social media
