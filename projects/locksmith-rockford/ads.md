## Ad Creative Guidelines

**Emergency campaigns:**
- Dark background (black or deep navy)
- Yellow or white bold text
- Phone number visible in the image itself
- Tone: urgent, fast, reassuring

**Non-emergency campaigns (car keys, lock change):**
- Clean, bright background
- Professional locksmith photo
- Tone: trustworthy, affordable, local

**All ads:**
- Always include "Rockford" or "Rockford Area" in the headline — local anchoring improves CTR significantly for service businesses
- Keep primary text under 125 characters for mobile preview (or accept truncation after line 3)
- Test 2 image variations per ad set minimum
