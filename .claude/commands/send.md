# /send

Send personalized outreach emails. Draft from prospect context, review, send, and auto-log.

Read `.claude/skills/send/SKILL.md` for full operations and guidelines, then follow the instructions there based on what the user asks for.

Supported operations:
- `/send [email] [subject] [body]` — Send an email with custom body
- `/send draft [prospect name]` — Draft personalized email from wiki context
- `/send log [prospect name]` — Log that you sent (auto-updates CRM and tasks)
