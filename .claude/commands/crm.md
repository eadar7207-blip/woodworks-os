# /crm

Track every conversation and interaction. Log calls, update contacts, flag follow-ups, and keep your pipeline moving.

Read `.claude/skills/crm/SKILL.md` for full operations and guidelines, then follow the instructions there based on what the user asks for.

Supported operations:
- `/crm log [prospect name]` — Log a call or interaction
- `/crm update [prospect name] [field] [value]` — Update contact info
- `/crm follow-up [days]` — Show prospects due for follow-up
- `/crm deal-summary` — Pipeline status snapshot
- `/crm contact [prospect name]` — Pull full contact record
